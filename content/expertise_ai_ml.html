As a co-founder and leader within Google's Capital Engineering (CapEng) team, I had the opportunity to contribute to strategic discussions around significant technical investments - particularly advising on the infrastructure needed to deploy and scale large AI/ML systems effectively at Google.


This often meant working closely with C-suite leadership and Board, including providing counsel to Alphabet's CFO, to help navigate decisions on capital allocation for these pioneering technologies. It's been a rewarding experience to play a part in shaping the infrastructure that supports such transformative advancements and to think about their long-term impact on Google's core businesses.
